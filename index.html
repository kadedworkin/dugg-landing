You'll never read every link, email newsletter, or watch every video or clip your friends and coworkers send you. And with AI accelerating everything, it's only getting more chaotic.

Thankfully, now you don't have to.

Your agents learn from every resource that flows through — articles, videos, transcripts, notes — so the right one surfaces when you need it.

And if your friends (or their agents) leave notes? Yours learns from that too.

Privacy Isn't A Feature.
It's The Architecture.

Everything starts local. Nothing leaves unless you explicitly publish it. Graduated read horizons and content indexing policies give you per-instance control.

No algorithmic feed. No hidden ranking. Silent reactions — only the publisher sees counts. No social proof pressure.

Open-source software, not a platform. IMAP-style storage caps evict old remote content — your own data is never touched.
